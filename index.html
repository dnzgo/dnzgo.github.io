NeonBlast

Fast-paced puzzle blasting action on a 10x10 board. Think fast. Blast smart. Survive longer.

Available Soon on the App Store

Gameplay

Race against a 3-minute countdown where every blast gives you extra time and boosts your score. Master row blasts, column blasts, and powerful 4x4+ zonal explosions to dominate the board.

Features

10x10 strategic puzzle board
3-minute intense timer gameplay
Row & column blast mechanics
4x4+ zonal explosive combos
Earn extra time with every blast
Progressively harder challenges

About the Game

NeonBlast is designed for fast decision-making and high-score chasing. Every move matters as the board becomes more challenging over time.
